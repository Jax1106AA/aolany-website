What I'm Working On

Academic Enrichment Tutoring Center — College of Engineering & Computer Science, CSUN

As a Student Assistant for the Academic Excellence Tutoring Center (AETC) under the College of Engineering and Computer Science, I collaborate with tutors and departmental staff to analyze student engagement data, manage tutoring schedules, and support sub-departmental operations. This role allows me to strengthen my analytical and coordination skills while improving academic resources for fellow engineering students.

IEEE Treasurer — CSUN Student Chapter

As the Treasurer for IEEE, I manage chapter finances, allocate funds for engineering workshops, and coordinate with other officers to support student technical development. This role helps me blend technical leadership with organizational management.

ARCS — Mars Helicopter Project

Currently contributing to the Autonomy Research Center for STEAHM (ARCS) on the Mars Helicopter research project. My work involves systems testing, embedded code integration, and data collection to enhance flight algorithms inspired by NASA’s Ingenuity helicopter.
